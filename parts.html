Best Part: BBa_K5123004 (LanM_D26W_N58W)

🏆 Best Composite CDS — LanM D26W/N58W

This double tryptophan variant is our best-performing part. It provides 2.3-fold enhanced Tb³⁺-sensitized luminescence compared to the best single variant (N58W), with a detection limit of 3 ppb and >99% selectivity for Tb³⁺ over competing REEs and transition metals. The variant retains picomolar Tb³⁺ affinity (Kd = 22 nM) and functions optimally at pH 2.5, matching e-waste leachate conditions. When fused with SpyTag, it enables recyclable Tb capture from real fluorescent lamp phosphor leachate with 85% recovery over 10+ cycles.
